Parts of keyboards

Layout

Base of a keyboard with varying sizes such as 60%, 70%, TKL, Full size, etc.

Switches

Mechanical components that register key presses. They come in various types, such as linear, tactile, and clicky, each offering a different typing experience.

Keycaps

The removable covers on top of the switches that users press. They come in various materials, profiles, and designs, allowing for customization and personalization of the keyboard.

Artisan Keycaps

Artisan Keycaps are specially created keycaps that are used as decoration to change up the look and add some detail as the user sees fit.

Keebs in the community

Keebs, or mechanical keyboards, have a vibrant and passionate community of enthusiasts who share a love for custom keyboards. This community is known for its creativity, innovation, and dedication to the craft of keyboard building and customization. Enthusiasts often gather on online forums, social media platforms, and at meetups to discuss their latest builds, share tips and tricks, and showcase their collections. The community is also known for its collaborative spirit, with members often working together on group buys for custom parts and sharing resources to help newcomers get started in the hobby.
